My Process

1. Discover & Research

I start every project with deep-dive briefs, competitor audits and audience mood boards. Understanding market gaps and emotional triggers lets me position visuals for maximum impact.

2. Concept & Iterate

Rapid sketching, colour exploration and high-fidelity mock-ups are shared in Figma. Feedback loops are compressed to 48-hour cycles so momentum never stalls.

3. Produce & Polish

Once direction is locked, I build final assets in Adobe CC and Figma, optimise for platform specs, and deliver organised source files with style-guides.

4. Launch & Measure

Post-launch, I stick around to A-B test thumbnails, tweak CTR-driven imagery and supply additional formats as your product evolves.

About Me

I’ve been crafting visuals since 2019, helping 250+ clients on Fiverr, Upwork and direct partnerships increase sales, build trust and look premium.

Philosophy

I treat design as a business language, not decoration. Every choice—colour, contrast, curve—must serve a strategic goal: higher clicks, longer retention, stronger recall.

Toolbox

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Figma, Webflow, basic React + Tailwind for dev hand-off. If you need 3-D, I fire up Blender; if you need motion, I graph in After Effects.

Metrics I track

Click-through rate, session duration, scroll depth, conversion rate, brand-recall uplift. Pretty is pointless if it doesn’t perform.
